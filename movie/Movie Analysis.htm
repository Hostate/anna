1.2 The course the disorder

The client’s disorder had started since ten years ago, he found that his mother had a relationship with a man, he worried about he would be abandoned by his mother. So he poisoned his mother and her boyfriend. In ordinary people’s mind, killing their own mother is a crime can’t be tolerant. There is no different to Norman, so he tried to erase his criminal, at least in his own mind. He did things followed. First, he stolen his mother’s body and kept it well. This was also the reason he loved those bird samples. Second, he began to think and speak for her, he gave her half of his mind and life. At times even he could speak to his “mother”. What’s more, the most scaring part in this case, was that he thought his mother can’t leave him like he did to his mother. Therefore, if he met a woman made him felt strong attraction, his “mother” personality felt jealous, so “mother” killed Marion and the other two girls. At the end of this film, Norman’s body was entirely dominated by his “mother” personality.

1.3 Past psychiatric illness and medical history

Before the client’s dissociative identity disorder, his mental was disturbed since his father was died. What’s more, I think he had suffered from strong Oedipus complex. He can’t leave his mother and unable to watch his mother had a relationship with other man and had the possibility and tendency to left him alone. That’s the reason he murdered his mother and her lover, and made the story go on.

It seems that Mr. Bates never went to hospital or had medical treatment to his mental situation.

1.4 Psychosocial history

Mr. Bates had a motel which was far from main road, so the motel did not have much costumer. He lived alone, did not met other people regularly. This circumstance was his own choice, because his “mother” personality didn’t love to meet other people times. But this lonely environment also made Ms. Bates’ personality become stronger and finally dominate the whole body.

Ms. Bates was a clinging, demanding woman, and she lived with her son in a world no one else existed. She loved to control her son’s live, even the girl his son loved with. This may be one of the reasons cause Norman’s Oedipus Complex.
